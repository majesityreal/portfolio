Cheeseburger King

This project was an amazing learning experience! I wanted to learn more about how hardware and software interact. With this game, I had direct access to memory and different registers in the GBA. I learned to use pointers to reference locations in memory. This project taught me a lot about low-level implementation of software. I had to implement 95% of everything from scratch, including the physics. I will never again take for granted the benefits of using a Game Engine.

Lore:

The King of Hamburg is dying. The whole kingdom is in mourning preparing for the loss of their favorite King. A servant comes to your house and tells you that the King has requested to speak with you specifically. What could he possibly want of you?

He explains that all his life he has had everything he has ever wanted. He ruled a magnificent kingdom, married the love of his life, and found solace in his mortality. But he explains that he has always felt that there was something missing, something he couldn’t quite pinpoint until now. He had a vision in the night of something new, something he calls the ‘Ultimate Sandwich’. He realizes that the thing that would make his life complete is one simple thing: a cheeseburger.

You, the King’s most trusted servant, are instructed to go on a quest to make this sandwich and satisfy the King’s last wish. He says that your Quest will revolutionize the sandwich industry, and that people will be telling your story for the rest of the ages. Obediently, you embark on this Grand Quest.
