Side By Side - Separation Across Dimensions

This game is heavily inspired by Super Paper Mario, incorporating the jump from 2D to 3D but with a co-op twist! As programming lead, my duties included assigning tasks to the programming team via ClickUp and monitoring our GitHub, merging Pull Requests. I consistently checked in with everybody to minimize merge conflicts and to ensure we remained on schedule. We utilized Agile methodology with weekly bite-sized sprints to best accomodate our busy student schedules.

I'm also very proud of the lasso mechanic I made. It varies the rotational speed based on distance and time. The longer you lasso, the faster you can launch the slimes.
