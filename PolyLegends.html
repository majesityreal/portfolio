PolyLegends - a learning experience!

Survive an endless wave of skeleton attackers! This was my first Unity game and a great learning experience! This really ignited my passion for game development. I spent many days over my winter break watching Unity tutorials just absorbing information. I was like a child presented with a huge room full of toys.

The player can toggle between three weapons: the shortsword, longsword, and bow. The shortsword has faster attacking but lower damage, whereas the longsword has a slower attack speed but higher range and damage. The bow fires arrows from a range. The scene resets when the player runs out of health.

I implemented a nav mesh for the skeleton to move towards the player. Every skeleton has a health bar which always points towards the camera.
